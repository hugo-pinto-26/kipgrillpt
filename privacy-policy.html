Our Privacy Policy

At KipGrill, we respect and value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, place an order, or interact with us in any way. By using our services, you agree to the collection and use of your data as described in this policy.

How We Collect and Use Information

  • 01. Personal Information:We collect your name, email address, phone number, and delivery address to process and fulfill your orders and communicate with you.

  • 02. Payment Information:Payment details are securely processed through our payment processor. We do not store your credit card information.

  • 03. Order Details:This includes the items you order, delivery preferences, and special requests to ensure accurate order processing.

  • 04. Usage Data:We collect IP addresses and browser information to improve our website's functionality and enhance user experience.

  • 05. Cookies:We use cookies to track site performance and personalize your experience. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of online transmission is entirely secure, and we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your information to third parties. However, we may share it with trusted service providers who assist in processing orders, delivering food, or other essential operations. Information may also be disclosed to comply with legal obligations.

Your Rights

  • 01.You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

  • 02.You can unsubscribe from promotional communications at any time.

  • 03.For any privacy-related inquiries, you can contact us directly.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Please review it periodically to stay informed about how we protect your data.
